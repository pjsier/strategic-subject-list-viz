ISR Stops and Count SSL Scores by Arrest Location

Description

The map shows the distribution of contact card stops by CPD in Chicago against the distribution of individuals with SSL scores by the census tract of their most recent arrests. The grouped dots are scaled by the number of contact card stops, while the census tracts are scaled by the number of individuals with their most recent arrest on the SSL in that tract.
