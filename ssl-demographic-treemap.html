SSL Demographics Treemap

Description

Treemap arranging sizes by count of individuals with SSL scores. Each rectangle represents a individuals grouped by race, sex, and age group, and sizes are determined by the number of individuals with an SSL score with those demographics. Some individuals are either listed as race "Unknown", which is grouped with American Indian/Alaskan Native due to very little representation.
